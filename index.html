I went to Sperreng, how can this be true?

I'm sorry to tell you this, but you didn't go to Sperreng. At least, not Sperreng Middle School. Students of the "school" are used by the facility as test subjects, like something out of Portal. In order to prevent these students from telling the world, while also letting them go home as to not create disappearances, Sperreng Research Facility has resorted to altering the memories of the people who go there. Any memory taking place inside Sperreng Middle School has been entirely fabricated by the facility. In fact, the structural interior of Sperreng Middle School does not actually exist. The inside of the building looks completely different than what students remember it to be, as the interior they remember is also a fabrication. This is why Sperreng has very little windows, so people can't see what's really going on inside.
